How to use the Personal Trainer Client Management System

  • To add a new client, click on "Add Client" in the navigation menu and fill out the form.
  • To view all clients, go to the Home page.
  • To edit a client's information, click the "Update" button next to their entry on the Home page.
  • To delete a client, click the "Delete" button next to their entry on the Home page.
  • To search for a client, use the "Search Clients" page.
  • To view VIP clients, go to the "VIP Clients" page.
